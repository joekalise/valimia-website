About

Built by someone with ankylosing spondylitis

Joseph, Valimia's founder, was diagnosed with AS a few years ago and spent a lot of time trying to make sense of his symptoms. The existing apps weren't built for AS. They didn't know what enthesitis is. They didn't track uveitis. They didn't connect sleep and step count to a pain score.

So he built one that does. Spondy tracks your daily symptoms, spots patterns in your health data, and warns you when a flare looks like it's building. It actually predicted a uveitis flare before it peaked, which is what convinced him to share it properly.

Spondy is made by Valimia. If you have ankylosing spondylitis or axial spondyloarthritis (axSpA), we hope it helps you the way it's helped us.
