About

Built for the complexity of fibromyalgia

Fibromyalgia doesn't behave like other conditions. Pain varies wildly day to day. Sleep quality, stress, diet, and activity all interact in ways that are hard to track in your head. Most health apps aren't built for this.

Fibro tracks your daily symptoms, spots patterns in your health data, and warns you when a flare looks like it's building. It connects brain fog to poor sleep, pain spikes to missed rest days, and mood dips to overexertion. These are patterns that only become visible once the data's in one place.

Fibro is made by Valimia. If you have fibromyalgia, we hope it helps you make sense of what your body is doing.
