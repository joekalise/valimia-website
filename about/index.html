About Valimia

Small team, specific conditions, built by someone who lives with them.

The apps

Valimia's first two products, Fibro and Spondy, are symptom trackers for fibromyalgia and ankylosing spondylitis. Both started as personal projects. I built them to track my own health data and bring something useful to my doctor's appointments, and they grew into apps other people with the same conditions found useful too.

Each app is built around the specifics of its condition rather than being a generic symptom log: Fibro understands flares, brain fog, and barometric pressure sensitivity; Spondy understands enthesitis, uveitis, and biologics. Both sync with Apple Health, spot patterns in the data automatically, and generate a report you can hand to your specialist.

Where we're headed

Valimia exists to formalize what were previously solo App Store projects. That means proper business structure and a foundation to build on as the apps grow.

We'll keep adding condition-specific tools where we think we can build something genuinely better than what's already out there, starting with the two conditions closest to home.
