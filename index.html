Why Valimia

Built by someone who understands chronic illness

Valimia started with two apps built to solve a personal problem: chronic conditions generate a lot of day-to-day data — pain, fatigue, flares, medications, sleep — and no good way to make sense of it before a ten-minute appointment with a specialist.

Fibro and Spondy exist because the existing options weren't built for the specifics of fibromyalgia or ankylosing spondylitis. We're a small company, but every feature ships because someone living with the condition it targets actually needed it.
